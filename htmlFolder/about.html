FAQ for Drop Services

What is drop servicing?

Drop servicing is a business model where you sell services to clients, but instead of providing the services yourself, you outsource them to freelancers or other service providers. Essentially, you act as a middleman, handling client interactions and project management while others do the actual work.

What types of services can be drop-serviced?

Common drop-serviced services include: Graphic design Web development Content writing Social media management SEO optimization Virtual assistance

Is drop servicing legal?

Yes, drop servicing is a legal business model as long as you comply with relevant regulations and tax laws. Make sure to have clear contracts with both clients and freelancers, and ensure that you are adhering to any legal requirements in your area.
